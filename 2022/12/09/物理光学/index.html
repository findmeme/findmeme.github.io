物理光学

从麦克斯韦方程组出发推导出光学中基本原理，如电磁波的形式……

麦克斯韦方程组

原始方程组
$$
\text{场方程}\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\nabla \cdot \vec{D}=\rho\
\nabla \times \vec{E}=-\frac{\partial \vec{B}}{\partial t}\
\nabla \cdot \vec{B}=0\
\nabla \times \vec{H}=\vec{j}+\frac{\partial \vec{D}}{\partial t}\
\end{array} \right. +\text{物质方程}\begin{cases}
\vec{D}=\varepsilon \vec{E}\
\vec{B}=\mu \vec{H}\
\vec{j}=\sigma \vec{E}\
\end{cases}
$$
积分可得边界条件
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
{D}{1\mathrm{n}}={D}{2\mathrm{n}}\
{E}{1\tau}={E}{2\tau}\
{B}{1\mathrm{n}}={B}{2\mathrm{n}}\
{H}{1\tau}={H}{2\tau}\
\end{array} \right.
$$

波动方程的导出

无源情况
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\nabla \cdot \vec{D}=0\
\nabla \times \vec{E}=-\frac{\partial \vec{B}}{\partial t}\
\nabla \cdot \vec{B}=0\
\nabla \times \vec{H}=\frac{\partial \vec{D}}{\partial t}\
\end{array} \right.
$$
写成E和H的对称形式
$$

$$
于是
$$
\nabla \times(\nabla \times \vec{E})=\frac{-\mu \partial \nabla \times \vec{H}}{\partial t}
$$
利用三叉乘公式得
$$
\nabla \cdot (\nabla \cdot \vec{E})-\nabla ^2\vec{E}=\frac{-\mu \partial}{\partial t}\left( \varepsilon \frac{\partial \vec{E}}{\partial t} \right) =-\varepsilon \mu \frac{\partial ^2\vec{E}}{\partial t}
$$
左边第一项为0，所以
$$
-\nabla ^2\vec{E}=-\varepsilon \mu \frac{\partial ^2\vec{E}}{\partial t}
$$
同理
$$
-\nabla ^2\vec{H}=\varepsilon \frac{\partial ^2\nabla \times \vec{E}}{\partial t^2}=\varepsilon \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left( \frac{-\partial \vec{B}}{\partial t} \right) =-\varepsilon \mu \frac{\partial ^2\vec{H}}{\partial t^2}
$$
整理可得
$$
\left{\begin{array}{l}
\nabla^{2} \vec{E}-\varepsilon \mu \frac{\partial \vec{E}}{\partial t}=0 \
\nabla^{2} \vec{H}-\varepsilon \mu \frac{\partial^{2} \vec{H}}{\partial t}=0
\end{array}\right.
$$
令$v=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\varepsilon \mu}}$，可得
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\nabla ^2\vec{E}-V^2\frac{\partial ^2\vec{E}}{\partial t^2}=0\
\nabla ^2\vec{H}-V^2\frac{\partial ^2\vec{H}}{\partial t^2}=0\
\end{array} \right.
$$
为标准的波动方程，特别的，真空情况下$c=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\varepsilon _0\mu _0}}$

无源传播的单色平面波

该波动方程有解，形式如下
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\vec{E}=\vec{E}_0e^{i(\vec{k}\cdot \vec{r}-\omega t)}\
\vec{H}=\vec{H}_0e^{i\left( \vec{k}^{‘}\cdot \vec{r}-\omega ^{‘}t \right)}\
\end{array} \right.
$$
或者实数解形式如下
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\vec{E}=\vec{E}_0\cos\mathrm{(}\vec{k}\cdot \vec{r}-\omega t)\
\vec{H}=\vec{H}_0\cos \left( \vec{k}^{‘}\cdot \vec{r}-\omega ^{‘}t \right)\
\end{array} \right.
$$
其中$k,w$的类比关系如下
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\omega ,T,\nu \Rightarrow 2\pi \nu =2\pi \frac{1}{T}=\omega\
k,\lambda ,f\Rightarrow 2\pi f=2\pi \frac{1}{\lambda}=k\
\end{array}
$$
为得到$k，w$的关系，我们将解代回波动方程
$$
\nabla ^2\vec{E}-\frac{1}{v^2}\frac{\partial ^2}{\partial t^2}\vec{E}=0\Rightarrow (i\vec{k})^2\vec{E}-\frac{1}{v^2}(-i\omega )^2\vec{E}=0
$$

$$
-k^2+\frac{1}{v^2}w^2=0\Rightarrow w=kv
$$

最终得到一个关系，这个关系称为色散关系
$$
w=kv
$$
==注==
$$
\begin{aligned}
\nabla \cdot \vec{E}&=\nabla \cdot \vec{E}0e^{i(\vec{k}\cdot \vec{r}-wt)}=\nabla iE{0i}e^{i\left( k_mr_m-wt \right)}\
&=E{0i}\frac{\partial}{\partial r_i}e^{j\left( k_mr_m-wt \right)}=E_{0i}e^{-jwt}\frac{\partial}{\partial r_i}e^{jk_mr_m}\
&=E_{0i}e^{-jwt}e^{jk_mr_m}jk_m\cdot \delta m_i\
&=E_{0i}jk_ie^{-jwt}\cdot e^{jk_mr_m}\
&=j\vec{k}\cdot \vec{E}\
\therefore \nabla \cdot \vec{O}&=i\vec{k}\cdot \vec{O}\
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
(\nabla \times \vec{E})_m&=\varepsilon _{mnl}\nabla _nE_l=\varepsilon {mnl}\cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial r_n}\cdot E{0l}e^{\left. i(k_jr_j-\omega t \right)}\
&=\varepsilon {mnl}\cdot E{0l}\cdot e^{-i\omega t}\frac{\partial}{\partial r_n}e^{i\left( k_jr_j \right)}\
&=\varepsilon {mnl}\cdot E{0l}\cdot e^{-i\omega t}\cdot e^{i\left( k_jr_j \right)}\cdot ik_j\cdot \delta _{nj}\
&=\varepsilon {mnl}\cdot E{0l}e^{i(\vec{k}\cdot \vec{r}-\omega t)}\cdot ik_n\
&=i\vec{k}\times \vec{E}\
\therefore \nabla \times \vec{O}&=i\vec{k}\times \overrightarrow{O}\
\end{aligned}
$$

E和H的关系（平面波）

将波动方程的解代回无源麦克斯韦方程组，
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\nabla \cdot \vec{E}=0\
\nabla \times \vec{E}=-\mu \frac{\partial \vec{H}}{\partial t}\
\nabla \cdot \vec{H}=0\
\nabla \times \vec{H}=\varepsilon \frac{\partial \vec{E}}{\partial t}\
\end{array} \right.
$$
利用两个替代关系
$$
\begin{cases}
\nabla =i\vec{k}\
\frac{\partial}{\partial t}=-iw\
\end{cases}
$$
得到
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\vec{k}\cdot \vec{E}=0\
\vec{k}\cdot \vec{H}=0\
\vec{k}\times \vec{E}=\mu w\vec{H}\
\vec{k}\times \vec{H}=-\varepsilon w\vec{E}\
\end{array} \right.
$$
由前两式可知$\vec{k},\vec{E},\vec{H}$成右手螺旋关系，于是有
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\therefore k\cdot |\vec{E}|=\mu w|\vec{H}|\
\therefore |\vec{E}|\cdot \frac{k}{\mu w}=|\vec{H}|\
w=kv=k\frac{1}{\sqrt{\varepsilon \mu}}\Rightarrow \frac{k}{w}=\sqrt{\varepsilon \mu}\
\therefore |\vec{E}|\cdot \frac{\sqrt{\varepsilon}}{\sqrt{\mu}}=|\vec{H}|\Rightarrow \sqrt{\varepsilon}|\vec{E}|=\sqrt{\mu}|\vec{H}|\
\end{array}
$$

球面波和柱面波

球面波（点光源）
$$
\vec{E}=\frac{\vec{A}}{r}e^{i(\vec{k}\cdot \vec{r}-\omega t)}
$$
可以这么记：能量守恒$I\propto \vec{E}^2$，于是
$$
\left(\frac{\vec{A}}{r}\right)^{2} \cdot 4 \pi r^{2}=\operatorname{const}
$$
柱面波（线光源）
$$
\vec{E}=\frac{\vec{A}}{\sqrt{r}}e^{i(\vec{k}\cdot \vec{r}-\omega t)}
$$

$$
\left( \frac{\vec{A}}{\sqrt{r}} \right) ^2\cdot 2\pi r\cdot h=\mathrm{const}
$$

折射反射定律

由边界条件
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
{E}{1t}={E}{2t}\
{H}{1t}={H}{2t}\
\end{array} \right.
$$
得
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
E_{i\bot}+E_{r\bot}=E_{t\bot}\
H_{i//}\cos \theta i-H{r//}\mathrm{c}\sin \theta r=H{t//}\cos \theta t\
\end{array} \right.
$$
由第一个式子得到
$$
E{i\bot}e^{i\left( \overrightarrow{k_i}\cdot \vec{r}-w_it \right)}+E_{r\bot}e^{i\left( \begin{array}{c}
\vec{k}r\cdot \vec{r}-w_rt\
\end{array} \right)}=E{t\bot}e^{i\left( \begin{array}{c}
\vec{k}_t\cdot \vec{r}-w_rt\
\end{array} \right)}
$$
这就要求
$$
\overrightarrow{k_i}\cdot \vec{r}-w_it=\vec{k}_r\cdot \vec{r}-w_rt=\vec{k}_t\cdot \vec{r}-w_tt
$$
由于$r，t$任意，所以
$$
\begin{cases}
\overrightarrow{k_i}\cdot \vec{r}=\overrightarrow{k_r}\cdot \vec{r}=\overrightarrow{k_t}\cdot \vec{r}\
w_i=w_r=w_t\
\end{cases}
$$
也即
$$
\begin{cases}
k_i\sin \theta _ix=k_r\sin \theta _rx=k_t\sin \theta _tx\
w_i=w_r=w_t\
\end{cases}
$$
结合色散关系
$$
w=kv
$$
得到
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
\frac{w_i}{v_i}\sin \theta _i=\frac{w_r}{v_r}\sin \theta _r\
\frac{w_i}{v_i}\sin \theta _i=\frac{w_t}{v_t}\sin \theta _t\
\end{array} \right.
$$
又
$$
n=\frac{c}{v}\Rightarrow \frac{1}{v}\propto n
$$
最终得到反射与折射定律
$$
\left{ \begin{array}{l}
n_i\sin \theta _i=n_r\sin \theta _r\
n_i\sin \theta _i=n_t\sin \theta _t\
\end{array}\Rightarrow \begin{array}{l}
\theta _i=\theta _r\
n_i\sin \theta _i=n_t\sin \theta _t\
\end{array} \right.
$$
